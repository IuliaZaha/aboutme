"Finding yourself"

  • Category: Animation
  • Tools: Unreal Engine 5.3, Blender, Motion Capture equipment
  • Project date: June, 2023
  • Project URL: Go to Youtube page

Storyline

Saddie is a trying to find their passion. They are looking for fulfilling the empty hole inside their soul through different hobbies or passion, trying to socially fit in. After a couple of failed attempts with several hobbies, Saddie finally finds a theatre stage with instruments and starts playing the cello. This is how Saddie finally finds their passion and fullfills the emptiness of their soul.

The process

This project is the result of a 4-students team.

  • 3D model creation
  • Motion Capturing
  • Retargeting
  • KeyFrame animations within the built Unreal Engine scenes
  • Cello scene finger animation adjustments in Blender
